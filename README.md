   # Market Royal: Stock Trading Game

   **Learn to trade. Zero risk. All fun.**

   Market Royal is a stock market simulation game where you trade virtual stocks, learn candlestick patterns, and compete on the leaderboard — all
   without risking real money.

   ## Download

   - **iOS**: [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/market-royal) *(coming soon)*
   - **Android**: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.marketroyal.app)

   ## Features

   - Trade 50+ simulated stocks across multiple sectors
   - Start with $100,000 in virtual cash
   - Adjustable simulation speed (1x, 10x, 20x)
   - Portfolio analytics with interactive charts
   - 20 candlestick patterns with visual guides
   - Technical indicators (RSI, MACD, SMA)
   - Global leaderboard and rankings
   - Beautiful dark-themed UI

   ### Frequently Asked Questions

   **Is this real trading?**
   No. Market Royal is a simulation game. No real money, real stocks, or real financial transactions are involved. All trading is 100% virtual.

   **Is the app free?**
   Yes. Market Royal is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden fees.

   **What data do you collect?**
   Minimal data for app functionality. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

   **What age do I need to be?**
   You must be 13 or older to use Market Royal.

   **Can I lose real money?**
   No. Everything in the app is simulated. You cannot win or lose actual money.

   **The app crashed / something isn't working**
   Please [open an issue](https://github.com/kashifcassandra/marketroyal/issues) with your device model, OS version, and a description of what
   happened. We'll look into it.
